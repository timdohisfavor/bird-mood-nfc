# Bird Habitat Design Notes

## Source Direction

The reference images use a clean nature-tech language:

- large white or pale-blue canvas
- soft landscape imagery at the bottom or behind the main phone frame
- strong black headline typography
- muted ecological green as the brand accent
- white cards with generous radius and restrained shadows
- occasional glass panels over nature scenes
- small pill labels and compact controls

## Mini Program Translation

- Keep Airbnb-like text hierarchy: black headlines, gray body copy, compact labels.
- Replace the flat gray page with a quiet habitat scene: sky, forest edge, wetland reeds, and park-like green landforms.
- Let the bird artwork stay foregrounded on transparent backgrounds.
- Use green as the primary action and status color; avoid saturated red unless it is a warning.
- Use white cards for readable content, with natural background visible around them.
- Use mild translucency only for hero/stat panels, not for dense text.

## Tokens

- Chinese font stack: `"PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", "Noto Sans CJK SC", sans-serif`
- page background: `#eef5f1`, `#f8faf7`
- text: `#121512`
- muted text: `#6f756d`
- primary green: `#2f6f3e`
- deep green: `#142f1d`
- border: `rgba(20, 47, 29, 0.12)`
- card: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.92)`
- radius: `28rpx` to `40rpx`
- shadow: soft, low opacity, vertical only
